1.In your local branch commit the modified changes.
2.Checkout 'master' branch.
3.Pull the master branch.
4.Checkout your local repository after pulling.
5.Go to 'logs' symbol, tick your local repository and master. Go to the first point and right click and select 'merge'.
6.Give 'create commit for merge' option. Resolove if there are any merge issues.
7.Push your changes to your local repository in the server.
8.Go to your local master and merge it with your local repository.
9.Push the changes in master.
